The roof of the hospital was fully prepared for the patient. However, Han Yoohyun had no intention of handing over his brother to others. Park Yerim stepped forward on behalf of Han Yoohyun, who had an air about him such that even Awakened people of lower rankings didn’t dare meet his eyes.

“He isn’t injured, so we can go like this. Please don’t bring in healers unnecessarily, just take us to a hospital room. You were told that potions can’t be used either, right?”

At Park Yerim’s words, the hospital staff stayed at a distance as they led them to a special private elevator. It was the ward where Han Yoojin had once been admitted in the past. Park Yerim made calls here and there as they headed to the hospital room. After informing Haeyeon of the situation, she briefly explained the situation to Yoo Myeongwoo and Noah.

“Should I tell Breaker too? -Sir?”

“Only let the guild leader know.”

“I should tell Chief Song-nim as well, right?”

Han Yoohyun nodded. In the meantime, the elevator stopped. Even before the doors opened, Han Yoohyun was scowling. Peace also growled quietly. Park Yerim got off the elevator first and beckoned, telling them to come quickly.

“I know you don’t like this, but it can’t be helped, you know. It’s not like ahjussi’s sick in a normal way.”

Park Yerim said quietly. There were no Hunter-dedicated hospitals other than Seseong’s. Other large hospitals had healers and systems for nullifying poisons, curses, and so on, but they weren’t specialized. Above all, there were many cases where there was a lack of understanding of special injuries caused by dungeons and skills. If nothing else, this place was the best choice for safety.

But after walking for a bit, Park Yerim’s brows also drew together slightly. She saw two people standing there, seemingly waiting, at the far end of the corridor. The hospital employees following jerked to a stop and stepped away. Bellaré flinched, its body wrapping tight around Park Yerim’s arm. Only Chirpie calmly flapped its wings as if saying hello.

“Han Yoojin-gun.”

A low voice rang through the corridor. Golden eyes were staring directly at Han Yoojin alone. If it had only been his gaze, it wouldn’t have been anything strange. But the pressure that filled the surroundings was openly picking a fight. Tension where it wouldn’t be strange if weapons were pulled out at any moment spread through the air.

Park Yerim stood in front of Han Yoojin as if to shield him and met Sung Hyunjae’s eyes. She didn’t yield an inch as their gazes faced off directly.
